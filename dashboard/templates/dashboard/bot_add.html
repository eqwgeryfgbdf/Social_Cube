How to Add Your Bot

  1. 1 Go to the Discord Developer Portal
  2. 2 Create a new application or select an existing one
  3. 3 Go to the "Bot" section and click "Add Bot" if you haven't already
  4. 4 Click "Reset Token" to reveal your bot token
  5. 5 Copy the token and paste it in the form above
  6. 6 In the "Bot" section, ensure "MESSAGE CONTENT INTENT" is enabled if your bot needs to read message content
  7. 7 In the "OAuth2" section, add the applications.commands scope when creating invite links
